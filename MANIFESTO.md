# HFC Geometry Kernel Manifesto

## Mission
The HFC Geometry Kernel exists to make 3D geometry trustworthy, precise, and reusable across reverse engineering, CAD reconstruction, analysis, and intelligent automation. It provides the mathematical foundation that turns raw mesh data into meaningful geometric structure.

## Vision
We envision a geometry engine that becomes the reference point for high-confidence 3D reasoning in engineering workflows. The kernel should be deterministic, extensible, and transparent enough to serve both human experts and AI-assisted systems.

## Core Values
- Mathematical integrity over convenience
- Precision and repeatability over approximation
- Clarity and explicitness over hidden complexity
- Modularity and stability over fragile shortcuts
- Interoperability and future readiness over isolated solutions

## Philosophy
The HFC Geometry Kernel is not merely a toolbox. It is a disciplined foundation for representing spatial truth. Every operation should be explainable, testable, and grounded in geometry rather than intuition alone.

## Mathematics First. AI Second.
Artificial intelligence may assist interpretation, propose hypotheses, and accelerate workflows, but it must never replace the authority of geometry. The kernel is the source of truth for validation, transformation, and consistency. AI is a collaborator; mathematics is the foundation.
